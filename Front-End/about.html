About Us

Welcome to the Radiology Department, where we use cutting-edge technology (and a healthy dose of guesswork) to peer into the shadowy depths of your anatomy. We're a team of highly trained professionals (at least more than a week), dedicated to providing you with the most... *interesting* diagnostic imaging experience this side of the uncanny valley.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make sure you don't have any extra organs you weren't aware of. We strive to provide accurate results (most of the time), so you can get back to your regularly boring life.

Our Team

  • Dr. Kareem (The Otterly Clueless): Our resident otter, whose diagnostic skills are inversely proportional to his ability to keep his lab coat clean.
  • Dr. Nihad (The Owl of Ominous Wisdom... and Questionable ACTIONS): Our enigmatic owl, who speaks in riddles and dispenses medical advice alongside dubious-looking candies.
  • Dr. Mohamed (The Bear Minimum): Our lovable bear, whose bedside manner is only slightly hampered by his tendency to accidentally perform good adjustments.
  • And a collection of other individuals who may or may not be human. We're not entirely sure.

Why Choose Us?

  • Equipment that's probably up to code (mostly).
  • A staff that's... enthusiastic.
  • Results that are... definitely something.
  • And an experience you'll never forget (for better or worse).
